About Us

FitnessTime offers participants a wide range of activities. Participants within the same age category are given the opportunity to work together on a team in an organized activity. Within one month of practice, teams are offered the chance to compete against each other in different areas: bodybuilding, weight lifting, running, etc. in a tournament that is held once every two months to bridge the gap between age categories. All teams are managed by an instructor. Each club has its own unique atmosphere adapted to different personalities in order to make everybody feel comfortable. For club membership, contact the club president for more information. Anybody who's interested (instructor or participant) must register beforehand in the "Register" section. We recruit five instructors every year through summer job interviews according to different criteria








Teenagers


A specially designed course for teenagers:street workout, weight lifting, running and even swimming + 40% discount for siblings.









Adults


A sport course split into 2 different categories: indoor and outdoor. Our gymnasium is equipped with state of the art materials while the outdoor course consists of long walks around the city.







Seniors


Through the tests we conducted, we concluded that swimming is very beneficial for middle-aged people. And that's why the senior sport activity is conducted in the water: either on the beach or in the pools.
